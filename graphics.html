    evaluation of web graphics

    Site evaluated: Sony

    purpose of graphcis on this site

    The graphics on the Sony web page are very interesting too me. I feel like the graphics are ment to represent the creativity and colorfulness of the Sony brand. It also shows the diversity of products they produce.It kind of a little complex though as they have so many images to look at, but overall though, the graphics really catch viewers eyes. If you were to click on one of the graphics it will send you to a different part of the website explaing that full graphic. Wether it's a video game, movie, camera, ect., the graphics on the Sony website really show what they are all about and what they are selling.

    Guidelines for effective use of graphics

    1. Yes- Graphics fit the purpose, organization, and style of the page.
    2. Yes- Website avoided using large graphics and has a quick load time
    3. Yes- The graphics help lead the viewrs' focus on to the most important content.
    4. No- The graphics are really cool and interesting, but they are pretty bright.
    5. Yes- Graphics haven't been used for textual content.
    6. Yes- alternate text is present witht the graphics.
    7. Yes- There is contrast between text and images.
